All six progressive disclosure patterns are valuable, but for an MVP I recommend focusing on **Phase 1: Hover Tooltips** and **Phase 2: Interactive Column Headers**. These deliver maximum transparency with minimal UI work:

1. **Hover Tooltips (Phase 1)**  
   – T-Shirt Badge: Tooltip with “Size M: Impact 3.6 + Effort 2.5 → Medium” and link to mapping rules  
   – Cost Range: Tooltip showing “3 people × £400/day × 8–16 weeks × 1.35 overhead = £111K–£223K”  
   – Timeline: Tooltip “8–16 weeks based on M-size buffer”  

2. **Interactive Column Headers (Phase 2)**  
   – Impact: Click header to show “Weighted average of Revenue, Cost Savings, Risk Reduction, Broker Experience, Strategic Fit”  
   – T-Shirt: Click to display current mapping rule thresholds  
   – Cost Range: Click to reveal “Role rates: Dev £400, Analyst £350, PM £500; Overhead 35%”  

These two enhancements:  
-  **Keep the table clean**—no extra icons or expanding rows everywhere  
-  **Provide context on demand**—users hover or click exactly where they need explanation  
-  **Are quick wins**—can be built using existing tooltip and modal components  

Once these are live, you can consider **Expandable Row Details** in Phase 3 for power users needing full scoring breakdowns.
