## Creating a New Use Case

### Step 1: Click "Add New Use Case"
Look for the blue button at the top right of any library tab.

### Step 2: Fill in Basic Information
**Required fields** (marked with *):
- **Title*** - A clear, descriptive name for your use case

**Recommended fields**:
- **Problem Statement** - What business problem does this solve?

See the [Scoring Your Use Cases](#scoring-your-use-cases) section below.

Click **"Create Use Case"** at the bottom. Your use case will receive a unique ID automatically (e.g., HEX_INT_001).

## Scoring Your Use Cases

Every use case is evaluated on two dimensions: **Impact** and **Effort**.

### Impact Score (1-5 scale)
Higher scores = Greater business value

Rate each factor from 1 (Low) to 5 (Very High):

1. **Revenue Impact** - Will this increase revenue?
   - 1 = No impact
   - 5 = Major revenue driver

2. **Cost Savings** - Will this reduce costs?
   - 1 = Minimal savings
   - 5 = Significant cost reduction

3. **Risk Reduction** - Will this reduce business risk?
   - 1 = No risk impact
   - 5 = Major risk mitigation

4. **Partner Experience** - Will this improve partner/customer satisfaction?
   - 1 = No impact
   - 5 = Transformative improvement

5. **Strategic Fit** - How well does this align with company strategy?
   - 1 = Misaligned
   - 5 = Perfect strategic fit

### Effort Score (1-5 scale)
Higher scores = More difficult to implement

Rate each factor from 1 (Easy) to 5 (Very Difficult):

1. **Data Readiness** - Is the required data available and clean?
   - 1 = Data ready to use
   - 5 = Data needs major work

2. **Technical Complexity** - How complex is the solution?
   - 1 = Simple implementation
   - 5 = Highly complex

3. **Change Impact** - How much organizational change is needed?
   - 1 = Minimal change
   - 5 = Major transformation

4. **Model Risk** - What's the risk if the AI model fails?
   - 1 = Low risk
   - 5 = Critical risk

5. **Adoption Readiness** - Are users ready to adopt this?
   - 1 = Users eager and ready
   - 5 = High resistance expected

### Automatic Calculation
The system automatically calculates:
- **Impact Score** (average of 5 impact factors)
- **Effort Score** (average of 5 effort factors)
- **Quadrant placement** (see Priority Matrix below)

### Manual Override
If you need to adjust the calculated scores:
1. Toggle **"Use Manual Scores"** in the detail view
2. Enter your custom Impact and Effort scores
3. Provide a reason for the override
4. Save your changes

## T-shirt Sizing

T-shirt sizing helps estimate the cost, timeline, and resources needed for each use case.

### Understanding Sizes
- **XS** - Very small project (2-4 weeks, 1-2 people)
- **S** - Small project (4-8 weeks, 2-3 people)
- **M** - Medium project (8-16 weeks, 3-5 people)
- **L** - Large project (16-24 weeks, 5-8 people)
- **XL** - Very large project (24+ weeks, 8+ people)

### How It Works
1. Open a use case detail view
2. Scroll to the **"T-shirt Sizing"** section
3. The system automatically suggests a size based on your effort score
4. Review the estimates:
   - Cost range (Â£ GBP)
   - Timeline (weeks)
   - Team size needed
   - Resource breakdown by role

### Resource Planning Table
The table shows:
- **Developer** roles and daily rates
- **Analyst** roles and daily rates
- **Project Manager** allocation
- **Total estimated cost** with overhead
- **Benefit calculations** based on impact score

### Adjusting Estimates
1. Click **"Override Automatic Size"** if needed
2. Select a different size
3. Provide a reason for the change
4. Save your selection

## Need Help?

### Common Issues

**Can't see use cases?**
- Make sure you're on the correct tab (Hexaware Internal, Industry Standard, or AI Inventory)
- Clear any active filters
- Try refreshing your browser

**Scores not calculating?**
- Ensure all 10 scoring levers are filled in (1-5 for each)
- Check that you've saved your changes
- Try refreshing the page

**Import failing?**
- Verify your Excel file matches the template format
- Check that all required columns are present
- Ensure score values are between 1-5
- Review the validation error messages

**Presentation upload not working?**
- Check file size is under 50MB
- Ensure file is in .pptx format
- Verify you have a stable internet connection
- Try a different browser if issues persist

For technical issues or questions about using the portal, contact your system administrator or IT support team.
